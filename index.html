UIUX Portfolio

Welcome to my portfolio! I am an undergraduate from Brown University graduating with a Bachelors of Art in Computers Science and a Bachelors of Art in Visual Art. The following portfolio focuses on four projects I have worked on this Fall to sharpen my UIUX skills.

Understanding the Consumer

Undergoing research, conducting interviews, creating a persona, and storyboarding is just scratching the surface in order to better understand your consumer and their needs! This project dives into the needs of a golfer when they purchase balls at a driving range and how I can improve their experience.

Rennovating a Fixer-Upper

A common UIUX task is to redesign an existing interface which is exactly what I did for this academic website outlining the production of papyrus. My goal was to keep the design simple yet aesthetically pleasing and modern.

Imagining a Future

After choosing a start-up we were passionate about, I worked in a group to imagine their future needs once operation starts. We iterated through multiple designs of an app for mushroom farmers using lo-fi Balsamiq and hi-fi Figma applications.

Every Action Has a Reaction

In a digital world with increasing digital interactions, I wanted to explore how React makes website design more interactive. Here I created a retail website for a hypotheical bakery in which customers can sort and filter items as well as add them to their cart!
